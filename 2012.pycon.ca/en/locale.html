PyCon.ca

Bringing the Canadian Python community together

November 9 – 13, 2012 • Toronto

You're invited to a kickoff conference, Nov 9-13 2012, in Toronto, getting us warmed up for the first PyCon North America conference outside the US - Montreal in 2014 and 2015! And when that's done, let's keep the momentum going with an annual PyCon in Canada.

And that's a wrap, PyCon Canada!

Here are the videos of the PyCon Canada 2012 talks to tide you over until next year!

Venue

Be there November 9-13, 2012 at Beverley Halls in downtown Toronto. A short walk from the subway, so when you aren't busy coding or chatting with your colleagues you've got lots of stuff to explore nearby.

Schedule

Start the fun Friday evening with a mix and mingle. Feed your brain at talks on Saturday, and if your sprint team is eager, you can start that night. Sprint or attend talks and tutorials on Sunday.
