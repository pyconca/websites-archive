    Vous êtes intéressé à devenir volontaire?

    We're looking for folks to help us run the registration desk, work the A/V gear, setup tables and chairs, corral speakers, and generally help us make PyCon awesome. Volunteers are free to attend the events in the evening, and will likely have time throughout the day to attend a talk or two.

    Apart from attending helping for at least one day of the conference, volunteers will be expected to attend an orientation session, which will take place on a weeknight in early November.

    If you are interested in volunteering, please fill out the volunteer form with your availability, or email volunteers@pycon.ca if you have any questions.
