Unata is an innovative, fast-growing startup that is transforming the customer shopping experience in grocery retail through highly personalized and interconnected in-store and online experiences.

We blend beautiful UX and UI with sophisticated data analysis and predictive technology, leveraging purchase behaviours to provide each customer with the most relevant and timely products and offers.

We use Python for everything here at Unata, from running learning algorithms on product datasets with Numpy, SciKits, NLTK and Pandas, to powering our APIs with Flask, and everything in between.

Our data persistence layer is built on SQLAlchemy, distributed task processing on Python-RQ and ZeroMQ, and our automated deployment management and other internal operations rely on Fabric. We use PostgreSQL as our general data store, as well as Cassandra, Redis, and Memcached where appropriate.

Our codebase is organized as an API-oriented architecture and split into several well-defined projects, all of which are hosted on GitHub.
