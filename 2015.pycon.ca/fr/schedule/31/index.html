Earthquakes and the Moon with Hany Fahim

Presentation on samedi at 2:50 après-midi to 3:20 après-midi in Room 1180.

Watch on YouTube.

Timekeeping is no easy business. This talk tells the story of how earthquakes and even the Moon force the concept of the "leap second", and the debacle in 2012 that took down most of the Internet. It also goes into some detail at how Python is handling this scenario.

Hany Fahim Bio

Hany Fahim is the founder and CEO of VM Farms, a 24x7 Operations Team and Cloud Provider. Hany has over 10 years of operations experience, both in building and supporting private and public cloud platforms, as well as Linux and OSS environments.
