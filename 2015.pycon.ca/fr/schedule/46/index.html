Building a Django app with Viewflow with Martin Hill

Presentation on dimanche at 12:20 après-midi to 12:30 après-midi in Room 1130.

Watch on YouTube.

Some applications, particularity those used in enterprise, have use cases the involve a workflow. Most web frameworks, Django included, don't address this specific need. Developers can build their own, but an ad-hoc approach can be hard to maintain. I'd like to share our experience solving this challenge using the Viewflow library.

Martin Hill Bio

Martin has worked as a software guy in too many industries: aerospace, biotech, media, and finance. In his current role as AdvisorStream's CTO, he finally has the power to select technologies. That power went straight to his head, and thus they adopted Python and Django.
